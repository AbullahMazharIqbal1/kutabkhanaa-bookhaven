Frequently Asked Questions

What are your shipping options?

We offer standard shipping (5-7 business days) and express shipping (2-3 business days). Free shipping is available on orders over $50.

Can I return or exchange a book?

Yes, we accept returns within 30 days of purchase in original condition. Exchanges are available for damaged or defective items.

Do you have a physical bookstore?

Yes! Visit our beautiful bookstore at 123 Book Street. We host regular events, book clubs, and author readings.

How can I suggest a book for your collection?

We love book suggestions! Use the contact form above or email us directly. Our curation team reviews all recommendations.
